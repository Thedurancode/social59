About Us

Experience Latin Cuisine
& Live Music

Located at 512 59th St, West New York, NJ 07093, Social59 offers an unforgettable experience with live music, a DJ, and amazing Latin cuisine. Our vibrant atmosphere is perfect for a romantic night out or a fun evening with friends.
